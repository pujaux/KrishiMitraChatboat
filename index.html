🔬 Disease Guide / रोग मार्गदर्शिका

🌿 Plant Diseases / पौधों के रोग

PLANT

Wheat Rust / गेहूं का रतुआ

गेहूं का रतुआ रोग

Orange-brown pustules on leaves. Spreads rapidly in humid conditions.

PLANT

Rice Blast / धान का झुलसा

धान का झुलसा रोग

Diamond-shaped spots on leaves. Major paddy disease in MP.

PLANT

Tomato Early Blight

टमाटर का अगेती झुलसा

Brown concentric rings on lower leaves. Caused by Alternaria fungus.

PLANT

Soybean Yellow Mosaic

सोयाबीन पीला मोजेक वायरस

Yellow mosaic pattern on leaves. Spread by whiteflies. Common in MP.

PLANT

Powdery Mildew / चूर्णिल आसिता

चूर्णिल फफूंदी रोग

White powdery coating on leaves. Affects wheat, peas, cucumbers.

🐄 Cattle Diseases / पशु रोग

CATTLE

FMD / खुरपका-मुँहपका रोग

खुरपका मुँहपका

Blisters on mouth & hooves. Highly contagious. Vaccinate every 6 months.

CATTLE

Black Quarter / काला बाय

काला बाय रोग

Sudden swelling of muscles. Fatal if untreated. Annual vaccination needed.

CATTLE

Mastitis / थनैला रोग

थनैला रोग (दूध की थैली की सूजन)

Udder inflammation. Reduces milk production. Very common in dairy cattle.

CATTLE

Hemorrhagic Septicemia

गलघोंटू रोग

High fever, difficulty breathing, swollen throat. Kills quickly. Vaccinate.

CATTLE

Lumpy Skin Disease

गांठदार त्वचा रोग

Nodular lumps all over body. Spread by insects. Vaccination available.
